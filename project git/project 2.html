Kimchi

fermented vegetables, primarily Napa cabbage, seasoned with spices like garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes, creating a tangy, spicy, and slightly sour flavor profile that is a staple in Korean cuisine.

tteokbokki

Tteokbokki are rice cakes simmered in a garlicky, sweet, and spicy gochujang sauce until velvety and tender.

Ramyeon

quick-cook noodle dish made from dried noodles, freeze-dried vegetables, and a powdered soup packet,rehydrate the noodles in boiling water eaten with eggs and green onions added.
